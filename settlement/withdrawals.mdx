## Verify and sign withdrawal wallets

Withdrawals only go to addresses you **register**, **electronically sign**, and that the team **approves**. On-chain “verification” here means **declaring ownership** via a document you sign in the app (DocuSeal), not connecting a hardware wallet in the UI.

### 1.1 Open the wallets area

1. Sign in and open the app.
2. In the sidebar, click **Exchange**.
3. Open the **Wallets de retiro** tab.

### 1.2 Add a new wallet

1. Click **Nueva wallet**.
2. **Select the network** for USDT (for example Ethereum ERC-20, Tron TRC-20, BSC, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, or Solana). The address format must match the network you choose.
3. **Paste the wallet address** exactly as shown in your external wallet or exchange. The form validates the format for the selected network.
4. Optionally add an **Etiqueta** (label), e.g. “Binance personal,” to recognize the wallet in lists.
5. Click **Continuar a firma**.

### 1.3 Sign the ownership document (DocuSeal)

After creating the wallet, you are taken to the signing page:

1. Wait for the embedded signing form to load.
2. Complete the **DocuSeal** flow (review the document and sign as instructed). You may be asked to use the **same email** as your HG.cash account; if the provider rejects your email, support must help.
3. When signing completes, you should see confirmation that the document was received and the wallet is **pending review** (**En revisión**).

If you leave the page before signing, you can return from **Wallets de retiro** and use **Firmar** on any wallet that is **Pendiente de firma**.

### 1.4 What happens next

| Status (UI) | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Pendiente de firma | You still need to complete DocuSeal. |
| En revisión | Signed; the team is reviewing the wallet. |
| Aprobada | You may select this wallet when creating a withdrawal. |
| Rechazada | Not usable; follow any communication from support. |
| Archivada | No longer available for new withdrawals (archiving is irreversible from the UI). |

When available, **Documento** opens the audit / signed document link. After approval, the wallet appears when you choose a **wallet de destino (aprobada)** for a withdrawal.
