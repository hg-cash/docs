## Before you begin

- **Access**: Exchange (USDT) must be **enabled for your account**. If you do not see **Exchange** in the sidebar or you see “no access,” contact support.
- **Language**: In the product, labels and messages are largely in **Spanish**; navigation paths below use the English route names (`/app/...`).
- **Support**: Errors such as rejected wallets, unsupported countries for spot schedules, or email rejected by the e-sign provider require **support**—those are not configurable in this guide.
